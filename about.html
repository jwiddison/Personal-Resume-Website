I'm a Jazz Musician

I love jazz music. I started playing the alto saxophone at age 11. Since then I've picked up soprano, tenor, and baritone saxophone, flute, piccolo, clarinet, and bass clarinet. I also play the piano and guitar. I love playing jazz for a lot of the same reasons that I love programming and development work. In jazz, the solos people play are improvised.

You have a framework of chord changes that you're allowed to play within. Once you understand that framework, you are free to navigate your way through it in any way you want. It's wonderful to have two outlets for the same type of creativity. Jazz and programming. I really believe that jazz deserves to hold a continuous place in America's popular music.
